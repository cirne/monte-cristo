Style for all images in The Count of Monte Cristo:

- Period-accurate 19th-century France and Mediterranean: dress, architecture, and atmosphere.
- Fine-art illustration style: like classic book engravings or oil studies—dignified, literary, no modern elements.
- Consistent lighting and palette: warm, slightly aged, with deep shadows and rich tones.
- No anachronisms, no photorealism; suggest character and place rather than literal realism.
